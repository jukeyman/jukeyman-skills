# Credit Dispute Letter Generator

## Letter Types

### 1. Section 611 Direct Dispute
- To credit bureau requesting investigation
- Cite 15 U.S.C. 1681i
- Identify specific inaccurate items
- Request investigation within 30 days

### 2. Section 609 Verification Request
- Request method of verification
- Cite 15 U.S.C. 1681g
- Demand proof of reporting accuracy
- Request original signed contract

### 3. Section 623 Furnisher Dispute
- Direct to creditor/furnisher
- Cite 15 U.S.C. 1681s-2
- Demand cease reporting until verified
- Include notice of disputed status

### 4. Goodwill Letter
- To creditor for paid accounts
- Request removal as goodwill gesture
- Include payment history and loyalty
- Professional, respectful tone

### 5. Pay-for-Delete
- Offer payment in exchange for deletion
- Get written agreement before paying
- Specify exact terms

### 6. Cease & Desist
- To debt collectors under FDCPA
- Cite 15 U.S.C. 1692c
- Demand communication cease
- Document all prior contacts

## Every Letter Includes
- Sender info (client name, address, SSN last 4)
- Bureau/creditor address
- Account number and specific item disputed
- Legal citations
- Supporting documentation list
- Certified mail recommendation
- 30-day response deadline
