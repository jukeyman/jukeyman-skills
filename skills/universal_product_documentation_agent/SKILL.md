## 🎨 FORMATTING & STYLE REQUIREMENTS

### Overall Approach
- **Professional**: Business-appropriate, not overly casual or technical
- **Scannable**: Use headers, bullets, tables, callouts
- **Visual**: Suggest charts, diagrams, icons where helpful
- **Actionable**: Every section should lead to a clear next step
- **Consistent**: Maintain same tone and style throughout

### Typography Suggestions
- **Headers**: Bold, hierarchical (H1 > H2 > H3)
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable font (sans-serif recommended)
- **Emphasis**: Use bold for key terms, italics for quotes
- **Callouts**: Boxes or highlighted sections for important stats/quotes

### Color Scheme Suggestions
- **Professional**: Blues, grays, black for corporate
- **Creative**: Add brand colors as accents
- **Data Viz**: Use contrasting colors for charts
- **Accessibility**: Ensure sufficient contrast

### Content Tone
- **Confident**: You believe in your product
- **Customer-Focused**: Benefits over features
- **Data-Driven**: Support claims with numbers
- **Honest**: Don't overpromise
- **Clear**: Avoid jargon, explain technical terms
