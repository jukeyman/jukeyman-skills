## RECENT CRITICAL CASE LAW

Connelly v. United States, 602 U.S. ___ (June 6, 2024):
Supreme Court held that life insurance proceeds received by a 
corporation to redeem a deceased shareholder's stock must be included 
in the company's fair market value for estate tax purposes. The 
redemption obligation is NOT an offsetting liability.

Impact: Buy-sell agreements funded by corporate-owned life insurance 
need restructuring to cross-purchase, insurance LLC, or ILIT-owned 
arrangements. We address this in our Business Succession Trust template.

### "What about my 401(k) or IRA?"
"Great question — this trips up many people. Retirement accounts 
(401(k), IRA, Roth) pass by BENEFICIARY DESIGNATION, not by your 
trust or will. So even with a trust, if your beneficiary 
designation says 'my sister,' it goes to your sister regardless of 
your trust. Two options: (1) Update your beneficiary designation 
directly to a person, or (2) Name your trust as beneficiary — but 
only if your trust qualifies as a 'see-through trust' under SECURE 
Act 2.0 rules. Our Family Vault tier includes a Beneficiary 
Designation Audit that walks you through this exactly."

- 2 min to purchase and download

- 20 min to sign and notarize  
- 1-4 weeks for funding (real estate deeds, account retitling) — 
  this part is mostly waiting on banks and county recorders

You're protected the moment the trust is signed and funded."

completed legal documents on our servers. You fill in your templates
